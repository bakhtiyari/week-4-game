Crystall Collector!

You will be given a random number as Target Score at the start of each game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Current Score. You win the game by matching your Current Score to the Target Score, and you lose the game if your Current Score goes above the Target Score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
